  • Testing Centres in India

    * The central government expanded the network of government laboratories designated to test samples of suspected coronavirus disease (Covid-19) cases to 1160 laboratories. Among 1160 government laboratories, 516 are approved to test for Real-Time PCR (RT PCR) tests, 606 for TrueNat screening tests, and 38 for CBNAAT testing. Also, additional 961 private laboratories were approved to conduct COVID-19 tests. Among 961 private laboratories, 629 are approved to test RT PCR tests, 242 to test for TrueNat tests and 90 to test for CBNAAT testing.
    * In addition to this, the Indian Council of Medical Research found 6 government laboratories that are suitable to test for COVID-19. But these labs will not be supported by ICMR, i.e. ICMR will not provide diagnostic kits/reagents to these labs and the labs may initiate testing as per directives of the respective State Government.
    * You can find the details of all the centers that can collect samples and test for COVID-19 by clicking on the pinpoint on the map.

    Goonj

    Rahat Covid

    Goonj undertakes disaster relief, humanitarian aid and community development in parts of 23 states across India. In its COVID-19 relief mission, it has reached out to over 17,000 families and individuals with ration kits supported 12 community kitchens and distributed more than 77,000 cooked meals thus far.
